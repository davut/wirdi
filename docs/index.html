Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Wirdi is thoughtfully crafted to help you build a consistent Quran reading habit without distraction.

Word-by-Word

Each ayah is displayed word by word with authentic Quranic script, helping you follow along at your own pace.

Dynamic Island Overlay

A beautiful notch-shaped overlay sits at the top of your screen — always visible, never in the way.

Three Reading Modes

Choose between speech-tracked highlighting, auto-scroll, or voice-activated scrolling to match your style.

Completely Private

No accounts, no cloud, no analytics. All processing happens on-device. Your reading stays between you and your Mac.

Reading Reminders

Gentle reminders at intervals you choose, with snooze support. Build a daily reading habit that sticks.

Multi-Display

Follow your cursor across displays or pin the overlay to a specific screen. Works beautifully with any setup.

Reading Modes

Read your way

Three carefully designed modes to accommodate different reading styles and preferences.

01

Word Tracking

Real-time word-by-word highlighting as you recite aloud. Arabic-aware fuzzy matching follows your voice naturally, even handling pronunciation variations.

Microphone
02

Classic

Smooth auto-scrolling at a constant, adjustable speed. No microphone needed — simply read along as the words flow across the overlay.

No Mic Needed
03

Voice-Activated

The overlay scrolls when you speak and pauses when you're silent. Perfect for reading at your own pace without manual controls.

Microphone

Install

Get started in seconds

Wirdi is free, open-source, and requires macOS 15 Sequoia or later.

Homebrew (Recommended)
brew install davut/wirdi/wirdi
or
Manual Download

Download the latest .dmg from GitHub Releases and drag Wirdi to Applications.

Download latest release

Works on Apple Silicon and Intel Macs.
